Analyse hops taken by an email to reach you

Email Trail

  • Analyse an email

    Email trail is generated by analysing email headers. Paste your email source below and click analyse. (Email source can be obtained by clicking "show original" or something similar in your mail client)

    • How does it work ?

      During transit each intermediary email server appends a Received header to your email, containing information such as hostname, protocol, time etc. This app extracts relevent information and presents it in a way that is easy to understand. The core functionality is available as a python package on GitHub .
